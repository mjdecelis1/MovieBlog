Movie Genres

Home Reviews Genres

Action

  • Mad Max: Fury Road (2015) - A high-octane chase through a dystopian wasteland. Starring Tom Hardy & Charlize Theron.
  • John Wick (2014) - A legendary assassin seeks revenge. Starring Keanu Reeves.
  • Gladiator (2000) - A betrayed general seeks vengeance. Starring Russell Crowe.
  • Die Hard (1988) - A cop fights terrorists in a skyscraper. Starring Bruce Willis.
  • The Dark Knight (2008) - Batman faces the Joker’s chaos. Starring Christian Bale & Heath Ledger.

Science Fiction

  • Interstellar (2014) - A team explores a wormhole for humanity’s survival. Starring Matthew McConaughey & Anne Hathaway.
  • Inception (2010) - A thief manipulates dreams. Starring Leonardo DiCaprio.
  • Blade Runner 2049 (2017) - A replicant uncovers a secret. Starring Ryan Gosling & Harrison Ford.
  • The Matrix (1999) - A hacker discovers reality is a simulation. Starring Keanu Reeves.
  • Arrival (2016) - A linguist deciphers alien communication. Starring Amy Adams.

Horror

  • Get Out (2017) - A chilling social thriller. Starring Daniel Kaluuya.
  • Hereditary (2018) - A family unravels dark secrets. Starring Toni Collette.
  • The Conjuring (2013) - Paranormal investigators confront spirits. Starring Patrick Wilson & Vera Farmiga.
  • A Nightmare on Elm Street (1984) - A killer haunts dreams. Starring Robert Englund.
  • The Shining (1980) - A man descends into madness in a haunted hotel. Starring Jack Nicholson.

Comedy

  • Superbad (2007) - Teenagers navigate a wild night. Starring Jonah Hill & Michael Cera.
  • Step Brothers (2008) - Two adult stepbrothers feud hilariously. Starring Will Ferrell & John C. Reilly.
  • Anchorman (2004) - A news anchor’s misadventures. Starring Will Ferrell.
  • Dumb and Dumber (1994) - Two dim-witted friends embark on a road trip. Starring Jim Carrey & Jeff Daniels.
  • The Hangover (2009) - A wild bachelor party leads to chaos. Starring Bradley Cooper & Zach Galifianakis.

Drama

  • Forrest Gump (1994) - A simple man influences history. Starring Tom Hanks.
  • The Green Mile (1999) - A death row guard meets a miraculous prisoner. Starring Tom Hanks & Michael Clarke Duncan.
  • Parasite (2019) - A poor family infiltrates a wealthy home. Starring Song Kang-ho.
  • The Pursuit of Happyness (2006) - A struggling salesman’s inspiring journey. Starring Will Smith.
  • Schindler’s List (1993) - A businessman saves Jewish lives during WWII. Starring Liam Neeson.

Romance

  • The Notebook (2004) - A timeless love story. Starring Ryan Gosling & Rachel McAdams.
  • Pride and Prejudice (2005) - A tale of love and misunderstanding. Starring Keira Knightley.
  • Titanic (1997) - A love story aboard a doomed ship. Starring Leonardo DiCaprio & Kate Winslet.
  • Crazy Rich Asians (2018) - A woman discovers her boyfriend’s wealthy roots. Starring Constance Wu & Henry Golding.
  • La La Land (2016) - A jazz musician and actress chase dreams and love. Starring Ryan Gosling & Emma Stone.
